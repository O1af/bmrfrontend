What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is an estimate of the amount of calories a person burns in a given day. It is a vital statistic which allows individuals to design a healthier, individualized diet and lifestyle. Given the growing obesity epidemic and the insufficient education on health, our BMR calculator is a critical tool in promoting education and awareness.
